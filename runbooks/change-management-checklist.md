Title: Checklist — Change Management (Ops)

Magic keywords

- KEYWORD: change-wattle-13
- TAG: CAB-LITE

Before change

- Clear objective + rollback plan written
- Blast radius assessed (region, customer segment, service dependencies)
- Monitoring in place (dashboards + alerts)
- Comms plan prepared (internal + external if needed)

During change

- Make one change at a time
- Observe key metrics for 10–15 minutes
- Record timestamps for each step

After change

- Confirm success criteria met
- Document what changed + where
- Schedule follow-up to remove temporary mitigations
